Privacy Policy for Wrist Paddle

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Wrist Paddle ("the App," "we," "us") is developed by Armrest Games. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and your choices.

Summary

Wrist Paddle is designed to collect as little data as possible. There are no third-party advertising networks, no user tracking, and no account creation required to play.

Information We Collect

Motion Data

The App uses your Apple Watch's built-in accelerometer and gyroscope to detect wrist movement, which controls gameplay. This motion data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never transmitted, stored, or shared anywhere. It exists only for the moment it takes to move your in-game paddle.

Gameplay Data

The App stores the following locally on your device (and, if enabled, synced privately via your personal iCloud account):

  • Your current score, rank, and progress
  • Unlocked skins, sounds, and customization preferences
  • App settings (sensitivity, sound preferences, control scheme)

This data is not accessible to us. We do not operate servers that receive or store your gameplay data.

Purchase Information

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. We may receive anonymized confirmation that a purchase was completed, solely to unlock the corresponding content on your device.

Game Center

If you sign into Game Center (optional), your best score is submitted to Apple's Game Center leaderboard under your Game Center player identity. This is handled entirely by Apple's GameKit framework under Apple's own privacy terms; we do not receive, store, or process your Game Center identity or any personal information from it on any server of ours (we operate no servers). We do not receive additional data from Game Center beyond what's needed to display your rank and achievements within the App.

iCloud

Your best score is stored in your personal iCloud key-value storage so it follows you across your own devices. This data lives in your iCloud account, is accessible only to you and this app, and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics

We may use Apple's built-in App Analytics (available through App Store Connect) to understand aggregate, anonymized usage patterns such as retention and drop-off points. This data is provided by Apple in aggregate form and does not identify you individually. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs.

Notifications

If you opt in to receive gameplay reminder notifications, this preference and the associated device token are used solely to deliver those notifications through Apple's Push Notification service. You can disable this at any time in the App's Settings or your device's system settings.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email, or other personal identifiers unless you voluntarily contact us for support.

Children's Privacy

Wrist Paddle is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not include user-generated content, chat features, or any way for users to communicate with one another.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since gameplay data is stored locally on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account), you can delete all App data at any time by deleting the App from your device. If you use iCloud sync, data may also be removed by disabling iCloud for the App in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in an updated "Last updated" date above.
